# Chunk 12 — Diet, Adherence & Insulin

[29:58] **Gardner Lab (Stanford):** Low-fat, keto, intermittent fasting — **if people stick to the diet**, all can produce deficit and weight loss.

[30:17] **Adherence** is always the issue. Use the plan you can **sustain**.

[30:43] Scientific advantage to **keeping insulin low** (doesn't require zero carb).

[31:15] Lower/moderate carb → lower insulin → positioned to oxidize more fat from exercise and at rest.

[21:06] If you must eat before exercise: **fat/protein** raise insulin less than carbs.
