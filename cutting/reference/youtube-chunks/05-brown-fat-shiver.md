- **White fat:** energy storage; must mobilize and burn elsewhere.
- **Brown fat:** between shoulder blades / upper back; mitochondria-rich; **thermogenic** — burns energy in place.
- **Beige fat:** intermediate.

[13:01] **Shivering** (not just cold) increases metabolism via brown fat.

[13:09] Shiver → muscle micro-movements → **succinate** release → brown fat thermogenesis → higher overall burn.
