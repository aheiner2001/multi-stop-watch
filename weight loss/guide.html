Your nervous system is the master controller of fat loss

Calories in versus calories out remains the fundamental formula—but how you mobilize and oxidize fat is governed by neural signals (epinephrine, shivering, NEAT) layered on top of energy balance. This app structures your cut around that science.

How Fat Burns

  • Mobilize — fatty acids leave fat cells (lipolysis)
  • Oxidize — fatty acids → ATP in mitochondria
  • Local epinephrine — neurons on fat tissue drive both steps; not mainly adrenal adrenaline in blood
  • White / brown / beige — brown fat (upper back) is thermogenic; shiver → succinate activates it

Medical Safety Guardrails

  • Physician first — Get cleared before fasted exercise, cold exposure, or aggressive deficits
  • Cold exposure — Extreme cold can shock the heart. Start ~60°F; adapt gradually
  • Prescription drugs — GLP-1 analogs (Semaglutide), Berberine, Metformin require medical supervision only
  • Sustainability — The best protocol is the one you can follow consistently
  • Lean mass — 140–160g protein + resistance; see Daily Hub and Maxes
