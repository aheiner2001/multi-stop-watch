# Chunk 09 — Caffeine

[23:37] Look to **behavioral tools first** before supplements — no magic pill.

[24:26] **Caffeine** — common, increases epinephrine from neurons innervating fat.

[24:39] Effective up to **400 mg** (~1.5–2 cups; cafes often ~1 g in medium — regular users get headaches without it).

[25:36] **Protocol:** **100–400 mg**, **30–40 min before exercise** — beneficial for fat oxidation (shifts fat vs glycogen % unless caffeine makes you train so hard you burn glycogen).
