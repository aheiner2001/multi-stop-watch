# Chunk 02 — Calories In, Calories Out & Nervous System

[1:17] **Core truth:** Calories in vs calories out is the fundamental and most important formula in fat loss and weight management.

- Ingest far more than you burn → likely gain weight (much as adipose tissue).
- Ingest fewer than you burn → lose weight; significant portion from body fat.

[2:10] A calorie is a unit of energy. Accept calories-ingested vs calories-burned — but **calories burned is strongly influenced by things you can control** that accelerate the proportion of adipose tissue you burn.

[2:36] Body fat is **innervated by neurons**. Neurons connect to fat and change the probability it will be burned.

[2:50] **Your nervous system is the master controller** and plays a strong role in the calories-out component.

**Tool:** Calorie tracker (food + daily log).
